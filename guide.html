Your 10-minute morning routine to never run out of viral fitness ideas
📊 Dashboard 📖 How to Use
What this is: Every morning this system automatically scans all of YouTube for fitness "what happens inside your body" Shorts that went viral, steals the winning hooks, scores fresh ideas for you, and writes ready-to-shoot scripts — so you wake up knowing exactly what to make. It runs by itself. You just read it and create.

☀️ Your daily routine (10 min)

1

Open the Dashboard

Every morning, open the Dashboard. It already refreshed itself — today's ideas are waiting. Nothing to run.

2

Read the top-scored topic

Look at “Today's Topics — Ranked.” The highest score (out of 100) is your best bet for today. Pick one you like.

3

Grab the hook + script

Scroll to “Ready-to-Shoot Scripts” — it gives you the exact first line (hook), the narration beats, the payoff, and what the 3D visual should show.

4

Refine in Claude (optional)

Paste the topic + hook into your Claude chat to polish the full script in your voice — then it's ready for your normal flow.

5

Produce & post

Generate the video (Google Veo/Omni), merge in CapCut, voiceover with ElevenLabs, post the Short. Done — repeat tomorrow.

🔥 The 2 patterns that win in your niche

VS  “[Exercise A] VS [Exercise B] — see what happens inside your body.” The strongest format. The viewer has to know which is better. (Running vs Walking hit 59M views.)

EVERYDAY  “What happens if you do [exercise] every day?” The habit hook — people are curious about their own routines.

Golden rule: The first 2 seconds decide everything. Your opening line must create a question the viewer can't scroll past. That's why the hook matters more than the animation.
